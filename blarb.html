About Me!

How this all came to be

Why?

Mental illnesses have always run in my family and I constantly see people struggling with it. Seeing this my whole life, I know how important of an issue this is. To me, this project is very important.

So many people struggle with mental health issues. As a teenager, I've seen peers go through it and even myself has struggled with mental health. In times likes this, sometimes all a person needs is someone to talk to.

How?

My vision was to create an AI model with characteristics of emotions and sensitivity to talk to that would have the looks of a small pet. This would help the person find the AI bot more easy to talk to. I also planned to eventually convert this into an app for easier use.

This is a hackathon and not everything can be programmed in one day. I programmed the key parts of what I wanted in this project. This has the potential to go far and I would like to develop it further given the chance!

Due to an efficiency constraint, I used OpenAI's Marv bot and pulled it as an API. This took many hours to figure out due t0 the inconsistency of converting a Curl file into Javascript and trying to send data back to the source. With the time left, I designed the html of the website, incorporating Bootstrap 5.

What after?

I plan to more thoroughly develop this till around June and expiriment with fine-tuning Ghat GPT when the API gets released. I also will work on a graphic 3d model to make this bot more visually appealing and easy to talk to!

At one point, I may request OpenAI for publishing the app and seeing how I can improve it after that!
